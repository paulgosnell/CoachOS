## Common Questions

### "Should I build the product first or content first?"
Content first. Validate demand, build audience, get indexed. Product takes 4-6 weeks anyway. Use that time to publish 10-15 articles.

### "How do I know if people will pay for this?"
You don't until they do. But indicators: waitlist signups, content engagement, beta usage, conversion from free to paid. Validate incrementally.

### "What if AI gets better and makes this obsolete?"
AI getting better makes this more valuable, not less. Better AI = better coaching. Your moat is context, positioning, and trust, not the AI itself.

### "Should I target all 3 personas or focus?"
Start with Overwhelmed Owen (solo founders). They have the most acute pain, fastest decision-making, and lowest acquisition cost. Scale Sarah after validation.

### "How do I compete with free ChatGPT?"
You're not competing with ChatGPT. You're competing with traditional coaches. ChatGPT is generic. You're specific, context-aware, and built for business coaching.
